Yoga with Reba

At Yoga with Reba, we believe in the transformative power of yoga to bring balance, peace, and wellness to your life. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced practitioner, we offer classes that cater to all levels.

Our Services

Sound Healing

Immerse yourself in a serene and transformative journey with our Sound Healing Yoga Class. This unique session combines the therapeutic power of yoga with the vibrational frequencies of sound healing instruments such as Tibetan singing bowls, crystal bowls, and gongs.

Chair Yoga

Discover the benefits of yoga in a gentle, accessible way with our Chair Yoga Class. This inclusive session is designed for individuals who prefer or need the support of a chair, making it perfect for seniors, office workers, those with limited mobility, or anyone looking for a mindful approach to movement.

Restorative Yoga

Enter a sanctuary of calm and rejuvenation with our Restorative Yoga Class. This deeply relaxing practice focuses on slowing down and opening your body through passive stretching, using props like blankets, bolsters, and blocks to support each pose and enhance your comfort.

Breath and Meditation

Reconnect with your inner calm and clarity in our Breath and Meditation Class. This class is dedicated to the art of mindful breathing and meditation, offering a serene space to explore techniques that cultivate awareness, reduce stress, and enhance your overall sense of well-being.

Mindful Meditation Break

Take a moment to pause and rejuvenate your mind and body with our Mindful Meditation Break. This brief, yet profound session is designed to fit into your busy day, providing a quick escape from stress and a refreshing dose of calm and clarity.

Yin Yoga

Dive into the tranquil and meditative practice of Yin Yoga, where stillness meets depth. This class is designed to stretch and rejuvenate the deeper connective tissues, promoting flexibility, circulation, and a sense of calm through long-held, passive poses.
